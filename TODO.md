Omarchy themes live at `~/.config/omarchy/current/theme/colors.toml` (18 colors). A bridge script needs to convert Omarchy's flat colors into a full M3 palette.

The `theme-set` hook already exists and fires on every theme change ‚Äî it just points to a missing script.

**Architecture**:
```

  ‚Üí reads colors.toml
  ‚Üí generates M3 palette (58 colors)
  ‚Üí writes scheme.json
  ‚Üí Colours.qml FileView auto-reloads
```
